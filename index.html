01 What is Gastrofest Batumi?

The city becomes a menu.

Gastrofest is a city-wide food festival format. For a limited time, selected venues each prepare one exclusive tasting set created specifically for the festival.

Every set is available at the same fixed price across all participating venues. Guests see the full list, choose where to go, show up, and order. No tickets. No passes. No complicated rules.

We’re bringing the format to Batumi for the first time. The idea is simple: turn familiar streets into a reason to explore cafés, bars, and restaurants people usually walk past.

The first edition will focus on coffee: around 15–20 coffee shops, each with one special coffee drink and dessert set, all at 25 GEL.

02 How it works

Three simple
routes.

The mechanics stay clear for every edition: find the venues, pick your route, order the set, vote for what you loved.

01

Browse the participants

Before the festival starts, we publish all participating venues, their sets, prices, and details. You know what you’re getting before you go.

02

Pick your spots

Choose one place or plan a full route. There’s no pass to buy and no booking ritual. Just arrive during the festival and order the set.

03

Discover something new

Try the café you always pass, the bar you saved months ago, or the restaurant you never had a reason to enter until now.

04

Vote for your favorite

Guests vote for the sets they loved most. At the end, we announce the top venues. Bragging rights included.

03 For restaurants and bars

A real reason
to be discovered.

Gastrofest is not a discount mechanic. It is a focused moment where your venue gets introduced to people who may not have found you otherwise.

New guests who actually show up

People tend to revisit the same familiar places. Gastrofest gives them a low-risk reason to try somewhere new.

Promotion you don’t build alone

Each venue gets a place on the website and in festival communication, with the set, details, and story presented clearly.

A creative marketing moment

You create one special set that shows your venue at its best instead of simply lowering the price of regular menu items.

Useful guest feedback

The voting mechanic gives you a clearer sense of what people noticed, liked, shared, and remembered.

First-edition visibility

The venues joining early help shape the first edition of a format Batumi has not seen before.

A standard worth keeping

Applications and sets go through a light review so the guest experience stays consistent across the festival.

Participation is limited. Not every application will be accepted. The review is there to protect the quality of the festival, the guest experience, and the venues that take part.
